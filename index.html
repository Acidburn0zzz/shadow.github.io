The Shadow Simulator

Shadow is an open source and unique discrete-event network simulator that runs real-life applications like Tor. Shadow combines the accuracy of emulation with the efficiency and control of simulation, achieving the best of both approaches.

Read more about Shadow, skip to the download page, or follow Shadow development on GitHub!
